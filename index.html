About Mama Lamula

A Healer Rooted in Tradition

Fort Portal, Uganda

Guided by Ancestors, Driven by Compassion

Mama Lamula is a renowned traditional healer and spiritual guide with over 30 years of experience, serving clients across the globe with powerful traditional healing services. Her authentic African methods have helped thousands of people in the United States, Europe, and beyond.

Born into a lineage of healers in the mist-laden hills of Fort Portal, near the legendary Mountains of the Moon, Mama Lamula inherited a sacred calling. She combines ancestral wisdom with herbal medicine to address physical, emotional, and spiritual challenges.

Her reputation as one of the best traditional healers has spread far beyond Uganda. Whether you are in New York, London, or Dubai, distance does not diminish the power of her healing — she offers remote consultations and rituals that work across any distance.

Mama Lamula believes that every problem has a spiritual root, and by addressing that root, true and lasting healing can occur. She works with clients of all backgrounds, respecting their beliefs while guiding them toward peace and prosperity.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does remote healing work?

Mama Lamula can perform rituals and send healing energy across any distance. She uses spiritual connection and ancestral power to work with you regardless of your location. All you need is an open heart and a sincere intention.

Is it confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations and rituals are handled with the utmost discretion. Your privacy is respected, and no personal information is ever shared with third parties.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary depending on the nature of the issue. Some clients experience immediate shifts, while others see gradual improvement over days or weeks. Mama Lamula will guide you on what to expect.

What do I need to bring to a consultation?

For in-person consultations, bring an open mind and a list of questions. For remote sessions, you may be asked to provide a photo or personal item to help establish a spiritual connection.

Can you help with health issues?

Yes, Mama Lamula offers herbal remedies for a variety of health concerns. However, she always encourages clients to seek conventional medical care when needed and uses her healing as a complementary approach.

Do you work with clients outside Uganda?

Yes, Mama Lamula serves clients worldwide. She has helped people in the USA, UK, Canada, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond — all remotely with great success.
