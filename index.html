About Mama Lamula

Ancestral Healer & Guide

Fort Portal, Uganda

Rooted in Tradition, Guided by Spirit

In the mist-laden hills of Fort Portal, near the legendary Mountains of the Moon, Mama Lamula continues a sacred lineage of spiritual healing and herbalism. She is a guide for those seeking to remove spiritual obstacles, heal from ancestral burdens, and find clarity in a chaotic world.

Drawing on the power of ancestral spirits and the wisdom of the earth's medicine, Mama Lamula provides holistic healing. Her practice is a sanctuary for those seeking answers — addressing illnesses attributed to ancestors, curses, and spiritual imbalance with compassion and profound insight.

“Someone is disturbing you.” — these are the words that often begin a journey of restoration. Through ancient methods of divination and herbal remedies, she unveils what is hidden and restores what is broken.

— Mama Lamula
What She Offers

Sacred Services & Remedies

Spiritual Divination

Using ancient methods — including the reading of sacred sticks and water — Mama Lamula unveils the spiritual forces affecting your life. She identifies ancestral spirits, removes curses, and provides guidance for your future.

Herbal Remedies

Harnessing the power of Uganda's rich biodiversity, Mama Lamula creates potent herbal remedies from roots, leaves, and bark to treat physical ailments, restore vitality, and strengthen the body's natural defenses.

Rituals & Cleansings

To remove negative energies, evil spirits, or the 'evil eye,' Mama Lamula performs sacred rituals that restore balance and harmony — cleansing your spirit, home, and life path.
